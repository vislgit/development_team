Welcome to Upright

Upright is a boutique photography firm specializing in modern and wildlife photography. With a focus on capturing the essence of nature and wildlife through a contemporary lens, we strive to convey the beauty and wonder of the natural world in every image we create.

Our small team of passionate photographers is dedicated to preserving and showcasing the diversity of wildlife while embracing modern techniques and aesthetics. Through our lens, we invite viewers to experience the awe-inspiring moments and intricate details of the world around us, sparking a deeper connection to nature and its inhabitants.

About Upright photography

Upright's history is one of passion, dedication, and a deep-rooted love for photography and the natural world. Founded by visionary photographers, Upright began as a humble endeavor to capture the essence of wildlife and nature through modern lenses. Over the years, our journey has been marked by countless adventures, from exploring remote habitats to witnessing breathtaking migrations. With each click of the shutter, we've strived to convey the timeless beauty and wonder of the world around us, fostering a legacy of excellence and inspiration in the field of photography.
